The Connoisseur's Conundrum

(It's a riddle, Monisha, not a "guess-guess" game.)

While the main event is momentarily paused for the culturally challenged, let's take a moment to evaluate your sense of taste. This has absolutely nothing to do with that... *common* treasure hunt. This is a test of true refinement.

I am born of Tuscan sun, a hard but elegant creation,
Twice-baked with almonds, for caffeinated conversation.
I don't crumble in espresso, that's a plebeian's mistake,
I soften with grace, for goodness' sake.
What am I?
